My projects

Here are some of the projects that I've participated in

Exi

Exi is a facemask designed in order to prevent covid-19 spread in Bogota's main public transport system: Transmilenio. This mask uses a three layered polypropylene filter that lets the user breathe while preventing them from inhaling virus particles. It also includes a silicone border to ensure face adjustment and comfort while preventing the virus particles from entering at the sides of the mask.

This project was made for the class IMEC-2700 during the 2020-20 semester at Universidad de los Andes in a group conformed by: Nicolás Fajardo Ramírez, Roberto Elías Cure Daza, Sara Urrea Sánchez, Mateo Nicolás Escobar Cerquera, Maria Juliana Pulido Otero and Juan Manuel Moreno Garavito.

Ratscape

Ratscape is a game made during a gamejam organized by Universidad de los Andes during december 2018. The game centers around a rat escaping a laboratory where it is being experimented on, and in order to escape it needs to collect coins and enter a portal while evading enemies and traps.

This game was made by a team conformed by: Santiago Serrano, Carlos Orduz, Nicolás Fajardo, Jose Daniel Fuentes and Santiago Gamboa
